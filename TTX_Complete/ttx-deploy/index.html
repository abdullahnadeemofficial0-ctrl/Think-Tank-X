The methodology
Evidence-based weighting — not intuition

The TTX framework weights each pillar based on empirical research into why early-stage startups fail. Team quality is weighted highest not because it sounds right, but because it is the single most consistent predictor of outcome across pre-seed and seed-stage ventures.

Each pillar score is the average of its questions, scaled to 100, then multiplied by its weight. The composite is fully auditable — every number traces back to a specific answer.

Composite = Σ (Pillar Score × Pillar Weight)
